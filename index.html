What

The Vision Dojo is an interactive art installation started as a project for the Israeli Burning Man event. A.K.A Midburn.

It allow the player to watch a colorful Realtime changing projection of his movements.
In front of every camera there can be up to 6 people in parallel. The installation is made out of projectors, special depth and high frame­ rate cameras. It requires ­a place to project on ­White wall / Lycra, Electricity. It operates at night time.
